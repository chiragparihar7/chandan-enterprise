WHO WE ARE?

  • We are pleased to introduce ourselves as UNIVERSAL WATERPROOFING WORKS concern established at Mumbai since 1985. We are undertaking Leak Detection Services, Waterproofing Services, Civil Services and Painting Services providing warranty on all the Waterproofing and Painting Jobs we carry out.
  • We are specialized into Terrace Waterproofing, Basement Waterproofing, Underground & Overhead Tank Waterproofing, Lift Pit Waterproofing, PU Grouting, Rising Dampness Waterproofing, WC/Bathroom Waterproofing, Swimming Pool Waterproofing and all type of construction chemical application all over India.
